Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025  ·  TrevBeats Multimedia

WriteTone is developed and operated by TrevBeats Multimedia. This Privacy Policy explains how WriteTone handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy — your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When setting up your voice profile, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Your name, brand name, professional name, and occupation — used only to build your AI voice profile
  • Social media handles (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X/Twitter, Facebook) — used to analyze your public post history
  • Profile photo — stored locally on your device only, never transmitted
  • Writing samples — pasted manually to improve voice fingerprint accuracy
  • Signature phrases — stored locally and used only when you toggle them on

Information Processed by the App

Text you enter for rewriting is sent to our AI proxy service to generate transformed output. This text is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers after a response is returned.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or payment information
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

All information you provide is used exclusively to power the App's AI voice-matching features:

  • Your profile data is used to build a personalized voice fingerprint stored locally on your device
  • Your input text and voice profile are sent to our AI proxy to generate rewritten content in your style
  • Your output history (last 20 rewrites) is stored locally on your device for your reference

3. Data Storage

WriteTone stores all data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and Keychain:

  • Your voice profile (name, handles, style preferences, writing samples)
  • Your output history (last 20 rewrites)
  • Your profile photo (if provided)

This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

AI Proxy Service

WriteTone uses a proprietary AI proxy hosted on Google Cloud (Firebase Cloud Functions) to process rewrite and voice analysis requests. Text processed by this service is not logged or stored by TrevBeats Multimedia beyond the duration of the request.

Perplexity AI (via Proxy)

Our AI proxy uses Perplexity AI's API to search public social media content and perform text generation. Only publicly available content is used. We do not access private accounts or private posts.

5. Camera and Photo Library

WriteTone requests camera and photo library access only to allow you to set a profile photo. Photos are resized and stored locally on your device only. Images are never transmitted to any server.

6. Children's Privacy

WriteTone is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you control your data retention. You may delete the App at any time to remove all stored data. There is no server-side data to delete or request.

8. Your Rights

Because WriteTone does not collect or store personal data on remote servers, most data rights are exercised simply by using or deleting the App. If you have questions about data processed through our AI proxy, you may contact us below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
